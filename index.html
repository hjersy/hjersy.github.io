ABOUT ME

I am a San Francisco-based user-centered UX/UI designer with front-end development skill on Web and mobile interface design. With the background of Web Design New Media and Computer and Communication Engineering, I strive to observe users’ needs and solve their problems and provide better solution with usability methods and knowledge of technology.

In the past year 2013, I was mainly focused on ux research and mobile design in a start-up company. My responsibilities are: participate in user research studies, create mockups and assets for prototypes, observe and make design changes to give the user a friendly interface. During the design process, my main functions include deliver flow diagrams, wireframes, mockups, and pixel specs either within requirements doc or as standalone specs. I also participate heavily in the actual production process including refining visual details, delivering pixel perfect assets and oversee the implementation.

As one of the UX/UI designers in production team, I worked collaboratively and closely with product manager and developers that practice heavily on agile development. The fast-paced environment enables me to constantly reiterate designs while collaborating with developers through out the development cycles.

Observe users’ need is my passion, solve problem and provide better solution is my purpose;
Meet user’s need and solve their problems make me happy.
